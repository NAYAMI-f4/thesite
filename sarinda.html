Special Dish

Special Kacchi Biriyani

Aromatic, flavorful and hearty, our signature biriyani is a feast for the senses. Steaming long-grain basmati rice is layered with succulent pieces of tender, marinated chicken or lamb, and topped with caramelized onions, fragrant saffron and fresh herbs. A blend of exotic spices, including cumin, cardamom and cinnamon, infuses the dish with complexity and depth of flavor, while juicy raisins and crunchy nuts add a touch of sweetness and texture. Served with a side of raita and papadum, this classic Indian dish is a must-try for any food lover.
